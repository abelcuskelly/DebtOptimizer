Chrome/Edge:

  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  2. Select "All time" or "Everything"
  3. Check "Images and files" or "Cached images and files"
  4. Click "Clear data"
  5. Refresh this page

Firefox:

  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  2. Select "Everything" in time range
  3. Check "Cache"
  4. Click "Clear Now"
  5. Refresh this page

Safari:

  1. Go to Safari > Preferences > Privacy
  2. Click "Manage Website Data"
  3. Click "Remove All"
  4. Refresh this page

Quick Alternative:
